What is FoxVision?

FoxVision is a product that will open the door to young VR (Virtual Reality) viewers everywhere with its design. Inspired by Google Cardboard, FoxVision keeps VR accessible but also adds a fox mask element. This simple addition will appeal to many.


Below is a short video that will get you up to speed with what the Google Cardboard is and does.

What inspired this?

This idea came about after the realization of how every VR mask up to date is a box. I began exploring what possible mask ideas could be attractive. Nothing seemed more obvious then the fox.
