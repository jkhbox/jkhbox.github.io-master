Overview

A restless world is linked with ours, but hidden. Governed by speed and data. This is the universe behind the web browser. It’s a harsh unforgiving space that is constantly growing and evolving filled with new technologies overlapping the old. Follow the path of XVL8 who is part of Google’s elite squadron of searcher droids developed to serve the unquestionable, User. The creator and destroyer. Discover a world with monolithic structures in the clouds, fall prey to salvage data hungry viruses in the shadows, and peer into the chasm of unlimited knowledge.
